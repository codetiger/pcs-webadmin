Key Features:

  • Intuitive Interface - A clean, user-friendly interface that simplifies the 3D design process. Navigate seamlessly whether you're just starting out or have years of experience.
  • Ready-to-Use Basic Shapes - Instantly access a wide variety of pre-built shapes and objects, perfect for quickly assembling models or starting projects.
  • Materials Library - Enhance your designs with an extensive library of materials and textures to bring your creations to life.
  • Camera and Lighting Controls - Adjust your camera angles and lighting setups effortlessly to create professional-quality 3D visuals.
  • Animation Capabilities - Bring your designs to life with simple animation tools, ideal for basic animations.
  • Real-Time Previews - See changes in real time as you work, with instant visual feedback, so you can fine-tune every detail without waiting for renders.
  • Export Options - Easily export your projects in various file formats to share on socials.and much more!
